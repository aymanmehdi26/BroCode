ACHIEVEMENTS

Major Events

Google I/O

Google I/O is an annual developer conference hosted by Google. It focuses on Google's latest software and hardware developments, including updates to Android, Google Assistant, Google Maps, and more. The event features keynote presentations, technical sessions, and hands-on experiences for developers.

Black Hat USA

Black Hat USA is a leading cybersecurity conference that brings together security professionals, researchers, and industry experts. The event focuses on the latest threats, vulnerabilities, and defensive strategies in the field of cybersecurity. It includes training sessions, briefings, and a vendor expo.
